About Patchwerk

A heritage of
thoughtful
capital.

Patchwerk operates as a family office with a clear focus on property and property-related investments. Like the craft of patchwork — where individual pieces are joined with precision to create something enduring — our portfolio is built through careful selection and long-term commitment.

Through our investment vehicle, Garlin ICAV, and our operating companies, we deliver returns that stand the test of time. We are patient, principled investors who believe that excellence in real estate demands both vision and rigour.

Our Approach

Precision
over
velocity.

We do not chase cycles or trends. Patchwerk's investment philosophy is grounded in long-duration thinking, deep sector expertise, and an unwavering commitment to asset quality. Each investment is carefully woven into a coherent whole.

01
Long-Term Stewardship

We invest with a generational horizon. Our goal is not short-term returns but assets that compound in value and purpose over decades.

02
Sector Depth

Property and property-related investments are our singular focus. This specialisation allows us to see opportunity where others see complexity.

03
Responsible Capital

From planning to operation, we hold each asset to exacting standards — for investors, communities, and the built environment alike.
